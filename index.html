World Overview

In a world where human consciousness has been weaponized into destructive energy, SEELE—meaning “soul” in French—surpasses physical force in raw power. But this power comes at a cost: draining one's soul means death. Mega-corporations monopolize this resource, shaping the fate of humanity.

A fugitive bodyguard, framed for her employer’s murder, is forced into hiding. To survive, she establishes a link with players in the real world. Through SEELE’s boundless awareness, she seeks to expose the conspiracy that binds her fate.
